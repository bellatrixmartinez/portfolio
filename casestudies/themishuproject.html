The Mishu Project

User Experience Flows, UI Design, Figma Prototype, Frontend Implementation (HTML + CSS), Materialize CSS, Responsive Design, GIT, Project Management

A portfolio to showcase Mishu's art and interior design projects.

IDEA AND BRANDING.

I first met Mishu a couple of years ago. Worked on the logo for her interior design consulting.

She contacted me this year to create her online presence and revamp her brand.

Mishu's brand evolved from interior design to incorporating her art. She creates very cool custom art pieces. From big canvas, to traditional chairs, and jean jackets.

She renamed it as The Mishu Project.

USER INTERFACE DESIGN.

For Mishu's online presence we went with a super minimalist bold look. Wanted to let her work have its highlight.

IMPLEMENTATION.

Her website is implemented in html and css using Materialize CSS as a framework.
